# Video Style Guide — Rut Adk Channel

## Style Selection

Pick the style based on the story type:

| Story Type | Style | Example Channels to Reference |
|---|---|---|
| New tool/product launch | Tutorial | Shaz M, Nate Herk, Leon van Zyl |
| Industry news / shift | Commentary/Analysis | Greg Isenberg |
| Personal experiment / test | Build-in-public | Nick Saraev |
| Business model / strategy | Commentary + Tutorial hybrid | Liam Ottley, Greg Isenberg |

---

## Style 1: Tutorial / How-To

**Best for:** Tool setup, automation workflows, step-by-step guides

**Structure:**
```
[0:00–0:30]  HOOK — Show the end result first ("Here's what I built in 20 minutes")
[0:30–1:30]  CONTEXT — What this tool is, why you'd want it
[1:30–Xmin]  WALKTHROUGH — Screen share, step by step, narrated clearly
             Break into numbered steps: "Step 1... Step 2..."
             Pause at gotchas: "Here's where most people get stuck"
[Xmin–Y]     RESULT — Show it working end-to-end
[Y–end]      BUSINESS ANGLE + CTA
```

**Tone:** Patient, clear, confident. Assume viewer is smart but not technical.

**Length:** 8–20 min

---

## Style 2: Commentary / Analysis

**Best for:** News events, industry shifts, "what this means for your business"

**Structure:**
```
[0:00–0:30]  HOOK — Open with the most surprising/counterintuitive thing
[0:30–2:00]  THE NEWS — What actually happened, stripped of hype
[2:00–5:00]  WHY IT MATTERS — The real implication for business owners
[5:00–8:00]  WHAT TO DO ABOUT IT — 2–3 concrete actions
[8:00–end]   CTA
```

**Tone:** Opinionated, direct. Don't hedge. Have a take.

**Length:** 6–15 min

---

## Style 3: Build-in-Public

**Best for:** Case studies, experiments, personal results

**Structure:**
```
[0:00–0:30]  HOOK — "I tried X for [time period]. Here's what actually happened."
[0:30–2:00]  SETUP — What I tried to do and why
[2:00–Xmin]  THE PROCESS — What I did, what broke, what worked
             Be honest about failures — this builds trust
[Xmin–Y]     RESULTS — Numbers, screenshots, outcomes
[Y–end]      LESSONS + CTA
```

**Tone:** Personal, honest, first-person. Share the messy reality.

**Length:** 10–25 min

---

## Title Formula

Write 3 title options per video:

**Curiosity:** Creates an open loop — "The AI Tool That [Unexpected Claim]"
**Benefit:** States the outcome clearly — "How to [Achieve Result] With [Tool]"
**How-to:** Direct and searchable — "How I [Did X] Using [Tool] in [Time]"

**Title rules:**
- Under 60 characters for full visibility
- Front-load the most important word
- No clickbait that the video doesn't deliver on
- Numbers perform well: "3 Ways...", "I Did X for 7 Days..."
