# RT's Profile — Relevance Scoring Guide

## Who RT Is
- 24yo, Thailand-based solo founder
- Building an AI + marketing consultancy targeting **businesses** (B2B) as clients
- Pre-revenue, pre-launch — needs first clients and content presence
- 5+ years crypto/Web3 marketing background
- Building YouTube channel "Rut Adk" for business owner audience
- Also involved in Phuket real estate venture (separate — lower relevance for AI brief)

## Scoring Criteria (1–10)

### 🔴 9–10 — Must Act On
Story is **directly sellable as a consulting service today** or **immediately unblocks a current bottleneck**:
- New tool/workflow that can be packaged as a client offering right now
- Content strategy or automation that solves RT's zero-content problem
- Case study showing exact business model RT is building (AI agency, AI OS for businesses)
- Something clients will ask about within the next week

### 🟡 7–8 — Important to Know
Story **builds credibility and expertise** RT needs to consult:
- New major tool/framework RT should understand to advise clients
- Infrastructure shift (e.g. MCP going mainstream) that underpins consulting conversations
- Business model evolution relevant to AI consultancy positioning
- Technical depth that differentiates RT in sales conversations

### ⚪ 5–6 — Market Context
Story is **good awareness** but not immediately actionable:
- Model releases (useful for client conversations, not urgently actionable)
- Funding news (validates the space, not actionable)
- Enterprise/large-company tools not relevant to RT's SMB-focused clients
- Research papers, academic developments

### ⚫ 1–4 — Low Relevance
- Hardware news, chip releases
- Consumer AI (gaming, entertainment)
- Unrelated industries
- Duplicate coverage of already-known topics

## RT's Unique Angles
- **Crypto/Web3 + AI intersection** — underserved niche, nobody owns it yet
- **AI for marketing specifically** — RT's strongest background
- **Non-technical business owner audience** — explain tools without jargon
- **Thailand/Asia angle** — regional AI adoption stories have extra relevance

## Scoring Notes
- Weight **actionability** over importance. A "world-changing" model release that RT can't use today scores lower than a workflow tutorial RT can sell next week.
- Weight **content opportunity** — does this story have an underserved YouTube angle?
- The Relevance Notes field should explain WHY each score was given, not just list the score.
